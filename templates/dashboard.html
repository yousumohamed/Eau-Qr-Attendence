For Teachers

  1. Login with your teacher account.
  2. Create a session from the Teacher Dashboard and pick the classroom, date and time.
  3. Display the QR code on the projector or screen in your classroom.
  4. Watch attendance update live and see who is present or absent.

For Students

  1. Login with your student account.
  2. Open your dashboard to see active sessions for your classes.
  3. Tap Scan QR and point your camera at the classroom QR code.
  4. Confirm success and check your recent attendance history.
